ArtDapp.

A decentralized fine art marketplace powered by Ethereum.

ArtDapp will utilize blockchain technology to change the way art is bought and sold:

  • Artists can sell their artwork on a public blockchain.
  • Ownership history and provenance is securely tracked and publicly recorded.
  • Artists have access to ongoing revenue streams when their art is re-sold.

ArtDapp will be funded by way of a public ICO in complience with applicable securities laws.
